# Epic 17: AoC 2015 Solutions II

**Status:** In Progress
**Parent:** [Phase 4 Meta Epic](./meta_epic.md)

## Overview
Solve Days 4, 5, and 6 of Advent of Code 2015.

## Milestones

### Milestone 17.1: Day 4 - The Ideal Stocking Stuffer
- **Input:** Secret key (string).
- **Logic:** Brute force MD5 hashes until one starts with "00000".
- **Requirement:** Epic 16 (Crypto), Loops.

### Milestone 17.2: Day 5 - Doesn't He Have Intern-Elves
- **Input:** List of strings.
- **Logic:** Check for vowels, double letters, forbidden substrings.
- **Requirement:** Epic 14 (Functions) - helpful for predicates.

### Milestone 17.3: Day 6 - Probably a Fire Hazard
- **Input:** Instructions for 1000x1000 grid.
- **Logic:** 2D Array manipulation.
- **Requirement:** Performance (1M elements).
