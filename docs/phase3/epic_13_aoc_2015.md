# Epic 13: AoC 2015 Solutions

**Status:** Planned
**Parent:** [Phase 3 Meta Epic](./meta_epic.md)

## Overview
Solve the first three days of Advent of Code 2015 to validate the compiler.

## Milestones

### Milestone 13.1: Day 1 - Not Quite Lisp
- **Input:** String of `(` and `)`.
- **Logic:** Loop, if/else, counter.
- **Requirement:** Epic 10 (Strings).

### Milestone 13.2: Day 2 - I Was Told There Would Be No Math
- **Input:** List of dimensions `lxwxh`.
- **Logic:** String split, parsing, math min/sort.
- **Requirement:** Epic 10 (Strings), Epic 11 (Math).

### Milestone 13.3: Day 3 - Perfectly Spherical Houses in a Vacuum
- **Input:** Directions `^>v<`.
- **Logic:** Coordinate tracking `x,y`.
- **Requirement:** Epic 12 (Maps).
